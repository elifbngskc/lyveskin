Labeling System

Skincare can be dangerous for some people, we are trying to minimize risk by labeling the ingredients.

We classify our ingredients by 3 categories; Safe, Neutral, Risky.

Safe ingredients are mostly natural and very low risk of irritating or damaging skin still should be checked for allergies.

We will be using color green or 🌿 emoji to show safe ingredients

Neutral ingredients are the middle ground. They have low risk of irritating skin yet not zero.

We will be using 😐 emoji to show neutral ingredients

Risky ingredients are mostly chemical and should be used in moderation, this ingredients can irritate skin and cause skin damage. For example to use some acids you need to gain immunity by using them little by little.

We will be using color red or 🧪 emoji to show risky ingredients
